Humble Shark

Private asset tracker for iOS and Android

Humble Shark is a private asset tracker for Malaysian users. Track your cash, EPF, stocks, crypto, property, and more — everything is entered by you, visible only to you (and people you explicitly share a book with), with no bank linking and no ads.
